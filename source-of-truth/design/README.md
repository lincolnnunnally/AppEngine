# Personal Exploration & Growth-Discernment Engine — design + content pack

The shared psychological-assessment / growth engine behind Kindred (friendship),
Aligned Souls (dating), Rebuilding Dads, Community Connections, and ChurchConnect.
Built to be the opposite of a shallow vibe-coded quiz: it discerns who a person is
(mindset vs. heartset, stage of change, meaning, the head–heart gap), meets them in a
non-linear journey, and paces challenge and reward toward flow — with **meaning
(Frankl / logotherapy) as its spine**.

Owner: Lincoln (ordained pastor · certified life coach · psychology degree · Frankl
student) — authored for owner review + tweak. Nothing is built from this yet.

1. **`discernment-engine.md`** — the framework: the states-not-traits dimensional model,
   triangulated anti-quiz assessment, flow-not-addiction engagement, the head–heart gap,
   the safety gate, cross-app configuration, and the phased build path. 7 owner decisions
   (D-a…D-g).
2. **`logotherapy-meaning-spine.md`** — Frankl integration: the will to meaning, the three
   value pathways, self-transcendence, tragic optimism, the existential vacuum, dereflection

   Dimension #11 (meaning / noetic). This is the spine the content is authored against.
3. **`kindred-sjt-item-bank.md`** — 18 keyed situational-judgment scenarios for friendship.
4. **`aligned-souls-sjt-item-bank.md`** — 18 keyed scenarios for right-fit dating (can two
   people build meaning and face suffering together — deeper than any dating app).
5. **`esm-prompts-and-journal-rubrics.md`** — the in-the-moment sampling prompts + the
   rubrics that read a journal for meaning-markers (not sentiment).
6. **`voice-and-output-copy-pack.md`** — the actual language the app speaks: pastoral warmth
   + coaching + Frankl's dignity, faith-present with faith-forward variants.
7. **`PANEL-REVIEW-notes.md`** — the expert critique (logotherapy scholar · clinical
   psychologist · formation director). P1 + key P2 fixes were folded into the files above;

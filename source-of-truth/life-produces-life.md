# Life Produces Life Product Doctrine

Life Produces Life is not only a ministry slogan. It is a design, business, product, and systems principle.

It guides how AppEngine designs apps, businesses, workflows, community systems, and growth loops.

## Pattern

Spark -> Serve -> Strength -> Best Life -> Multiply Life

## Meaning

1. Spark: A person or business receives an idea, hope, need, opportunity, or conviction.
2. Serve: The spark becomes action by helping, serving, building, or solving a real problem.
3. Strength: Through useful action, the person, team, business, church, or community gets stronger.
4. Best Life: The system begins working well. The people providing service experience better life, and the people receiving service experience better life.
5. Multiply Life: The solution grows, improves, teaches others, opens new channels, reaches new people, or reproduces itself.

## Important Correction

Do not treat service or sharing as only the final step after someone has hope or healing.

Service can be the beginning of healing. A person may find purpose, hope, identity, and strength by becoming useful and helping someone else.

## Applies To

- Ministry apps
- Business apps
- Community apps
- Internal workflow tools
- Customer service systems
- Growth strategy

## Product Questions

Every app should answer:

1. Who is stuck or underserved?
2. What spark starts movement?
3. What small act of service or action can move them forward now?
4. How does that action strengthen the user, team, business, church, or community?
5. What does Best Life look like in this app's specific context?
6. How can the solution multiply life without forcing the app to become something outside its charter?
7. What must this app not become?
